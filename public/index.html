How to Use

  • Allow GPS access when prompted by your browser
  • Your current location will be automatically detected
  • Enter a destination (address or coordinates: latitude,longitude)
  • Click "Calculate Route" to find the fastest path
  • Route line color shows traffic: Green (light), Yellow (moderate), Orange (heavy), Red (very heavy)
  • Location and traffic updates refresh automatically every 10 seconds
  • Arrival time adjusts based on real-time conditions
